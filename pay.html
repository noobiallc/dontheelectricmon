Common Questions

Payment FAQ

Is it safe to pay with my card online?
Yes — all card payments are processed through Stripe, which is trusted by millions of businesses including Amazon, Shopify, and Google. Your card information is encrypted with 256-bit SSL and is never stored on our servers. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified, the highest level of security certification available.
Will I receive a receipt after paying?
Yes. As soon as your payment is processed, an email receipt is sent automatically to the email address you provide at checkout. Keep it for your records. If you don't receive it within a few minutes, check your spam folder or call Don at 770-256-2754.
Can I pay with Zelle, Venmo, or Cash App instead?
Absolutely. If you prefer to avoid card processing fees or just find it easier, you can pay via Zelle at 770-256-2754, Venmo @DonElectricMon, or Cash App $DonElectricMon. Please include your name and invoice number with your payment so Don can match it to your job.
What if I need to pay a partial amount?
No problem. Call or text Don at 770-256-2754 and he'll arrange a custom payment link for the specific amount, or you can use Zelle, Venmo, or Cash App for a partial payment. Just make sure to note your name and invoice number.
I paid but something looks wrong — who do I contact?
Call or text Don directly at 770-256-2754 or email dontelectricmon@gmail.com. Please have your payment receipt handy. We'll resolve any issues quickly — Don values your business and wants to make things right.
